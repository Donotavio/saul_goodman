Ah, e não podemos esquecer das redes sociais. Em vez de rolar infinitamente o feed, que tal usar esse tempo para aprender algo novo? Oh, and let’s not forget social media. Instead of endlessly scrolling through your feed, how about using that time to learn something new? Sure, this will make you feel like an alien on a planet full of procrastinators, but who cares, right? The important thing is that you’re *moving*.

Now, let’s get to the point. The truth is, most of us love talking about change, but when it comes time to act, we prefer to stay in snooze mode. It's easier to think that luck will smile upon us than to actually do something about it. So, if you’re willing to take the plunge and maybe do something real in 2026, go for it! Just remember: change only happens if you actually work for it. And if you decide to stay in your comfort zone, don’t worry. The couch and the chips will always be there for you.
